About Me

Hi, This is Lindsay.

I'm the voice, author, and creator behind "Taste of Toung". What started as a casual hobby over 14 years ago in 2010 while I was working as a fourth grade teacher has now grown into a full-blown business (!!) that reaches millions of people with fun recipes each month, with content that has been featured on The Kitchn, CNN, Refinery29, Brit + Co, POPSUGAR, Huffington Post, The Everymom, PureWow, and more.

What to Expect:

On my blog, you’ll find a delightful mix of recipes, from quick weeknight dinners to indulgent desserts. I focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients and strive to make cooking approachable for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned chef or just starting out, there’s something here for you!

Favorites:

I’m particularly fond of experimenting with global cuisines, so expect to see recipes inspired by my travels, family traditions, and a sprinkle of creativity. I also love sharing tips on meal prep, kitchen hacks, and the joy of cooking with loved ones.

Join the Community:

I believe cooking is all about connection. I’d love for you to join my community of food lovers! Feel free to share your experiences, ask questions, or connect with me on social media. Let’s inspire each other in the kitchen!
